  1. Aggregate Functions

    • Agregatsiya funksiyalari bir necha qator ustida hisob-kitob olib boradi va bitta natija qaytaradi.

  2. COUNT(): Counts rows.

  3. SUM(): Calculates the total sum.

  5. MAX() and MIN(): Find the highest and lowest values.

[!NOTE] SQLda HAVING clause GROUP BY orqali guruhlangan ma ºlumotlarni filtrlash uchun ishlatiladi. WHERE clausedan farqli o‚Äòlaroq, HAVING faqat guruhlash va agregatsiya (masalan, COUNT(), SUM(), AVG(), MAX(), MIN())dan keyin qo‚Äòllaniladi.

Syntax:
